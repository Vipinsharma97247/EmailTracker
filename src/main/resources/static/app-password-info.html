App Password is used for sending mail to different mail accounts, it is different than your mail password and more safe too.

..Steps to generate App Password..

Step 1:

Go to myaccount.google.com/apppasswords and choose your mail account and enter your password.

Step 2:

Create your App Password.

Step 3:

Name your App Password.

Step 4:

Copy you App Password as this password will not be recovered after closing.
